A Place I Would Like to Visit

Given the choice, I would visit Greece. In Ancient times, this region was the envy of the world, as its progressions in Philosophy, technology, and literature, were far more advanced. Pythagoras, who was one of the first great mathematicians and philosophers. Socrates, possibly the greatest philosophical thinker in the history of the world. Plato, a great interpretor of Philosophy. These are just a few of the wonderful minds, and legends, that emerged from the Greek culture. Greece also had the worlds largest library of both formal, and modern books (of that time), called the Library of Alexandria. For these reasons, I want to see the nation that brought about a higher degree of knowledge and understanding to our world, today.
